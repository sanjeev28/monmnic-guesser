Why This Demonstrates Security

Each BIP-39 mnemonic phrase is derived from 256 bits of entropy, creating 2²⁵⁶ (approximately 10⁷⁷) possible combinations. Even at high speeds, it would take longer than the age of the universe to check all possibilities.

This demonstrates why:

  • Brute-force attacks against BIP-39 mnemonics are computationally infeasible
  • The security of cryptocurrency wallets comes from mathematical impossibility, not obscurity
  • Real security risks come from poor storage practices, not the cryptography itself
